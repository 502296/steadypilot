Not in the beta. Manual entry is enough to start. Integrations can come later.

No. Frequency adapts to risk. Stable restaurants get monthly guidance; pressure triggers weekly support.

Practical actions: margin protection, staffing pressure, pricing/portion tuning, cost control, and cash stability.
